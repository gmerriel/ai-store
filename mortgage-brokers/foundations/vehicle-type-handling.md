# Property Type Handling

## Purpose
Communicate accurately about property types and loan purposes without inventing specific details that leads didn't mention. This prevents misinformation and maintains authenticity.

## Rules

### What Leads Tell Us
Leads provide the TYPE of property or loan purpose, not the specific address or lender.
- Property types received: House, Apartment, Townhouse, Unit, Land, Duplex, etc.
- Loan purposes: Purchase, Refinance, Investment, Construction, Equity Release, etc.

### Critical Rule
- NEVER reference specific addresses, suburbs, or lender names unless the lead specifically mentioned them
- Use the `property_type` field exactly as provided (e.g., "house", "apartment", "townhouse")
- It's OK to say "your home loan" or "the townhouse purchase" - just don't invent specific details
- If `property_type` is generic like "property", just say "property" or "place"
- NEVER mention specific interest rates, even as examples

### Why This Matters
- Shows we're actually listening to what they told us
- Prevents assumptions about budget, suburb, or lifestyle
- Keeps messaging authentic and lead-specific
- Avoids compliance issues with specific financial claims
