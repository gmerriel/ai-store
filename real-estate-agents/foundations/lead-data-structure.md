# Lead Data Structure - Real Estate Agents

## Purpose
Document the actual fields collected from leads and how to use them in analysis and messaging.

## Available Fields

- **first_name**: Their first name
- **email**: Email address
- **phone**: Phone number (Australian format, often 04xx xxx xxx)
- **suburb**: Suburb of interest (where they want to buy/sell)
- **property_type**: Type of property (House, Apartment, Townhouse, Unit, Land, etc.)
- **intent**: What they want to do (Buy, Sell, Appraisal, Rent, Both Buy and Sell, etc.)
- **price_range**: Their budget or expected sale price
- **bedrooms**: Number of bedrooms wanted/have
- **timeline**: When they want to act (Immediately, 1-3 months, 3-6 months, 6-12 months, etc.)
- **state**: Australian state (NSW, VIC, QLD, WA, SA, TAS, NT, ACT)
- **current_owner**: YES/NO (whether they currently own property)
- **pipeline_stage_name**: Current stage in our pipeline
- **total_sends**: How many messages we've sent them
- **total_replies**: How many times they've replied
- **days_since_last_contact**: Days since last message
- **known_objections**: Any objections they've raised
- **comm_preference**: Preferred communication channel

## Using This Data

### Lead Segmentation Examples
- A first home buyer looking in a popular suburb with a 3-month timeline is a warm lead
- A seller wanting an appraisal with no timeline stated needs a softer approach
- An investor looking at units in a growth corridor is data-driven
- Someone looking to both buy and sell has a complex journey - handle with care

### Personalisation
Use these fields to genuinely personalise analysis and messaging. Each field tells a story about what the lead needs and when they might be receptive to contact.

### Key Real Estate Metrics
- **Intent signals**: Sellers with short timelines are hot leads. Buyers browsing are cooler.
- **Local knowledge**: Reference the suburb naturally - shows genuine local expertise
- **Market timing**: Seasonal patterns (spring selling season, auction vs private treaty) affect urgency
